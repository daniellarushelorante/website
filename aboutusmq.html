ABOUT US!

“MUSICQUEST”, is a user-friendly and interactive mood music website where you can play music that depends on your mood. As the user that is using the website enjoys the music that they are listening to, they can engage in games/quizzes that will challenge their own knowledge about the artist/s or singer of the song that you are listening to. By using this website the users can temporarily escape from reality, they can use this mood music website to take a break from being stressed to reality.
